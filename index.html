About Me

Scholar. Builder. Connector.

Dr. Jayakumar Sadhasivam is an Associate Professor Grade 1 at the Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) in the School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCOPE). He holds a Ph.D. in Information Technology from VIT.

With expertise spanning Open Source Programming, Network Security, Storage Technologies, and Machine Learning, Jayakumar focuses on leveraging technology in education and developing open-source software that addresses the unique needs of learners.

As a long-standing Mozilla volunteer, he has contributed across six major projects — from quality assurance to localization — and founded the Mozilla Firefox VIT Club to nurture the next generation of open-source contributors.

Track 1 — Career Strategy & Operations

Assistant Director, Career Development Centre

Architecting career outcomes for 10,000+ students through strategic industry partnerships, data-driven placement frameworks, and scalable automation models across South India. Transforms raw campus data into actionable intelligence for recruitment and career readiness.

Track 2 — Academia & Cybersecurity

Associate Professor, SCOPE

Teaching web programming, network security, storage technologies, and machine learning. Deep specialization in malware analysis and cybersecurity, with active research in AI-driven education transformation.
